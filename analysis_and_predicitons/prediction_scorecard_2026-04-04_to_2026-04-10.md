## Executive Summary

- **Attritional baseline (48% “most likely”)** matched **Apr 4–6** well—deadline rhetoric, GCC/Israel damage, **Israel** deep strikes (**South Pars**, airports, **Sharif**-area reporting), **Oman**/**Pakistan**/**Egypt** diplomatic churn, **Hormuz** coercive signaling (**IRGC** tanker claim **04-04**, toll/fee narratives).
- **Apr 7** delivered a **major miss** relative to the written distribution: a **two-week U.S.–Iran ceasefire** announced **<2h** before the **Tuesday** deadline, **U.S.** **offensive** halt on **Iran** (with **defensive** carve-out), **Islamabad** talks scheduled, and **sharp** oil **relief**—closer to the **15%** de-escalatory branch than to “diplomacy noisy but behavior stays warlike” for the **bilateral** Iran–U.S. axis.
- **Lebanon** became the **spoiler theater**: **U.S./Israel** asserted the deal **did not** cover **Hezbollah** fighting; **Pakistan**/**Iran** read included **Lebanon**—**Apr 8** saw the **heaviest** **IDF** **Lebanon** wave of the war (**MOH** **182** dead **Wednesday** per **AP**; **civil defence** **254** per **Reuters**), **Hormuz** still **closed** per shippers, and **Iranian** claims of **breached** **10-point** conditions—**right direction** on “Israel outside main U.S.–Iran channel widens targets” but **underweighted** **magnitude** and **immediate** **post-announcement** timing.
- **Infrastructure apocalypse** branch (**bridges/power “civilization”** rhetoric) **did not** fully execute: **partial** **bridge**/**Kharg**/**petchem** strikes **Apr 7** and sustained **pressure**, but **no** **full** **grid** campaign; scores as **threat-intense** week with **Right Direction / Wrong Mechanism** on **how** escalation **paused** (**mediator**+**deadline** **swerve** vs **pure** **kinetic** **follow-through**).
- **Hormuz “middle state”** (**depressed** flow, **non-normalized** **commercial** **recovery**) **held**: even after the **ceasefire** headline, **Windward**/**Reuters**-style reporting showed **~11** transits **04-08** and **minimal** **24h** **throughput** **04-09** vs **~140**/day pre-war; **Apr 10** **asymmetry** (**Iran** **own** **shipping** vs **foreign** **hemmed**).
- **Missed / under-modeled**: **Farsi/English** **10-point** text **mismatch**; **Japan** detainee **bail**; **Saudi** **East-West** pipeline strike **claim** **04-08**; **U.S.** **CPI** **+0.9%** **March** tied to war (**04-10**); **Hashd**/**Houthis** remained **secondary** in briefs (**Pakistan** **Yemen** ceasefire **mention** **04-09**)—consistent with **low** **base-rate** calls.

## Actor-By-Actor Review

### United States

- **What was forecast:** Sustained Iran strikes; elevated infra risk if threats bind; **minimize** negotiation visibility; selective Hormuz diplomacy; possible DOD shock (low).
- **What happened:** Strikes through **Apr 7** then **offensive** **halt**; **maximal** **infra** **rhetoric** with **partial** **kinetic** **follow-through**; **highly** **visible** **Pakistan-mediated** **two-week** **truce** and **Vance**-led **talks** **path**; **Trump** **social** **pressure** **04-10**.
- **What was missed:** **Speed** and **visibility** of the **deadline** **pivot**; **underweighting** **mediator** **convergence** (**Pakistan**/**Sharif**/**Munir**) as **hard** **branch** changer.

### Israel

- **What was forecast:** Deep Iran strikes; Lebanon expansion; friction with U.S. energy/Hormuz messaging.
- **What happened:** **Deep** **Iran** **strikes** **until** **U.S.–Iran** **pause**; **Lebanon** **campaign** **intensified** to **record** **single-day** **toll** **04-08**; **explicit** **Lebanon** **exclusion** from **ceasefire** vs **mediator** text.
- **What was missed:** **Immediate** **post-deal** **Lebanon** **spike** as **primary** **week** **story** alongside **Hormuz** **dispute**.

### Iran

- **What was forecast:** GCC/Israel salvos; conditional IRGC corporate lane; reject humiliating terms; Oman-style Hormuz tactics without normalization.
- **What happened:** **Salvos** and **infrastructure** **pressure** **early** week; **SNSC** **accepted** **truce** while **framing** **victory**; **strait** **control**/**fee** **narrative**; **Qalibaf**/**Araghchi** **preconditions** **04-08–10**.

### Hezbollah

### Hashd

- **Over-weighted** **Trump** **presentation** **as** **persistently** **kinetic-first** **for** **the** **full** **window**; **under-weighted** **deadline** **reversals** and **mediator** **bridging** **documented** in **same-week** **briefs** (**Pakistan** **04-06**).

- **Treated** **de-escalatory** **branch** as **needing** **clean** **strike** **lull** **first**; **actual** **path** was **messy** **pause** **with** **ongoing** **fires** **and** **Lebanon** **surge**.
- **Negotiation-signal** **error**: **conflated** **UNSC**/**process-heavy** **breakthrough** **unlikely** with **all** **breakthroughs** **unlikely**—**bilateral** **mediated** **tracks** **can** **move** **faster**.
- **Timing**: **Correct** **material** **for** **Apr 4–6**; **Apr 7** **inflection** **compressed** **into** **one** **evening** **vs** **week-long** **assumption**.
