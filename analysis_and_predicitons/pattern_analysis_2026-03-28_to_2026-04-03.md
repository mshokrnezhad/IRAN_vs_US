- **Pattern assessment:** **Performance- and instinct-heavy** crisis management (consistent with `who_is_trump.json` themes: **audience calibration**, **toughness signaling**, **adaptation**, **“go with the punches”**) layered on **continued military execution**. **Rhetoric and operations diverged** enough that **behavior**—strikes, reinforcements, infrastructure hits—should weigh more than **optimistic** negotiation claims (per lessons).
