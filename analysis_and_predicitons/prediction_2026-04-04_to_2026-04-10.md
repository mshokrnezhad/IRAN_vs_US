## Executive Summary
- **Most likely:** The war stays a **high-tempo air–missile–maritime endurance fight** with **multiple diplomatic tracks** (U.K.-led Hormuz forum follow-through, Oman–Iran transit ideas, residual Pakistan/China messaging) **running in parallel** to **continued strikes**—**not** replacing them. **Hormuz** remains a **middle state**: **some** politically filtered or fee-like passage **signals**, **not** normalized commercial flow.
- **United States:** Expect **sustained strike operations** with **elevated** probability of **bridge/power/desalination-class** targets **if** the president maintains the **post-address** threat sequence; **public** lines may **shift quickly** with **audience feedback** (consistent with performance-heavy, instinct-driven decision style in `who_is_trump.json`), including **minimizing** negotiation emphasis when **framing strength** after **F-15** loss.
- **Iran:** **GCC energy/water** and **Israel** pressure **continues** in **bursts**; **IRGC** **corporate/university** threats may produce **isolated** incidents or **narrow** kinetic probes **or** remain **mostly** rhetorical—**kinetic follow-through** remains **conditionally** likely, not baseline-certain (per lessons).
- **Oil:** **Post–Good Friday** sessions risk **gap repricing** toward **prompt scarcity** if **headlines** stay hot; **IEA/strategic release** talk remains a **policy shock absorber**, not a substitute for **strait** mechanics.
- **Israel:** **Deep strikes** inside Iran and **Lebanon** operations **continue**; **public** **Litani**-south framing **increases** risk of **UN** and **allied** friction **without** stopping the campaign.
- **Houthis:** **Episodic** **long-range** fires or **Red Sea** risk messaging **more likely** than a **sustained** second **front** matching Hormuz scale—**early-window activation** already observed; **persistence** is the open question (per lessons).
- **Fast-moving invalidators:** **Confirmed** **crew capture** and **broadcast** PSYOP, **second** U.S. manned loss, **major** **Kuwait/Saudi/UAE** refinery **outage**, or a **surprise** **tactical ceasefire** with **matching** **strike silence** would **rapidly** shift scenario weights.
