# Daily War News Brief - 2026-03-13

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-03-13
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; specific event times are listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary
- The United States publicly escalated pressure on Iran's leadership by offering up to $10 million for information on Mojtaba Khamenei and other senior IRGC-linked officials.
- A large explosion struck central Tehran during a state-organized Quds Day rally, even as senior Iranian officials appeared in public in a show of defiance.
- The visible appearance of Ali Larijani, Abbas Araghchi, and other officials undercut some U.S. claims that Iran's leadership was fully underground and unable to coordinate.
- The day’s main signal was political and informational rather than diplomatic: Washington intensified leadership-targeting pressure while Tehran emphasized public resilience.
- No credible de-escalation breakthrough was visible; instead, symbolic and psychological warfare against the opposing leadership remained central.
