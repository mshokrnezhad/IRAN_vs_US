# Daily War News Brief - 2026-03-04

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-03-04
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; specific event times are listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary
- Iran launched new missile attacks on Israel and U.S. bases as the U.S. and Israel widened their campaign and Israel opened a new strike phase in Lebanon.
- U.S. officials said Iran was firing fewer missiles than at the start of the war and announced plans to strike progressively deeper inland in Iran.
- The U.S. military said an American submarine sank an Iranian warship near Sri Lanka, while AP and Reuters presented it as a major symbolic escalation in naval warfare.
- The U.S. Senate backed Trump by blocking an effort to limit his war powers, reinforcing the administration's political room to continue the campaign.
- Reported cumulative deaths passed 1,000 in Iran, topped 70 in Lebanon, remained around a dozen in Israel, and stood at six for U.S. troops.
- The conflict remained open-ended, with shifting U.S. and Israeli language on regime change, war duration, and the next Iranian supreme leader.
