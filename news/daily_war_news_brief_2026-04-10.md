## Executive Summary

- **JD Vance** departed **Friday** for **“make-or-break”** **U.S.–Iran** talks in **Pakistan**, warning **Tehran** not to **“play us”** while saying he expected a **positive** negotiation if **good faith** exists (**Reuters**).
- **Iran** raised **last-minute** **preconditions**: **Qalibaf** on **X** said **Washington** had agreed to **unfreeze** **assets** and to a **Lebanon** ceasefire—talks should **not** start until fulfilled; **FM** **Abbas Araqchi** demanded an **end** to **Israeli** **Lebanon** airstrikes (**Reuters**). **Pakistani** sources said both officials were expected in **Islamabad**.
- **Trump** posted that **Iran** has **“no cards”** beyond **short-term** **Hormuz** **“extortion”** and that **“the only reason they are alive today is to negotiate!”** (**Reuters**—paraphrase/quote per wire).
- **Pakistan** **PM Shehbaz Sharif**, in a **Friday** national address, called the next phase a **“make-or-break”** step toward a **permanent** ceasefire (**Reuters**).
- **Lebanon** fighting continued: **Friday** strike on a **government** building in **Nabatieh** killed **13** **state security** personnel per **President Joseph Aoun**; **Hezbollah** claimed **rocket** salvos (**Reuters**). **Reuters** also cited **Lebanese authorities**: **≥1,953** killed since **Mar 2** (in-story update).
- **Strait asymmetry**: **Reuters** reported **Iranian** **ships** moved **unimpeded** **Friday** while **many foreign-flag** vessels remained **hemmed** inside—**oil** disruption called worst on record; **U.S.** **March** **CPI** **+0.9%** tied to war **inflation** channel (**Reuters**).
