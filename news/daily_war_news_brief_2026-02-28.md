# Daily War News Brief - 2026-02-28

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-02-28
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; specific event times are listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary
- The United States and Israel announced the start of a joint military campaign against Iran, with President Trump calling it "major combat operations in Iran" and Prime Minister Netanyahu calling it `Operation Lion's Roar`.
- Israeli and U.S. officials said the operation targeted Iranian Revolutionary Guard, Basij, ballistic missile, and related military targets across Iran.
- Multiple high-confidence reports said Supreme Leader Ali Khamenei was likely killed, but same-day attribution remained mixed: Trump declared him dead, Netanyahu said there were growing signs he had been killed, and Iranian state confirmation appeared after the covered day.
- Iran retaliated with missile attacks on Israel and on Gulf states hosting U.S. forces, including Bahrain, Qatar, Kuwait, and the UAE; Jordan also reported interceptions.
- Reuters reported one death in Abu Dhabi from the retaliatory strikes, while Reuters-linked reporting said the Pentagon reported no U.S. deaths or combat-related injuries on the day.
- International reaction was cautious: European leaders pushed for renewed talks, Oman condemned the U.S. action, and the U.N. Security Council scheduled an emergency meeting.
