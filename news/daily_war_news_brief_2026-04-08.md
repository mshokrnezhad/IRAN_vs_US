## Scope

- Date covered: 2026-04-08

## Executive Summary

- **Oil** crashed **below $100/bbl**: **Reuters** (**New York**, **2026-04-08**) reported **Brent** **-$14.52** to **$94.75** and **WTI** **-$18.54** to **$94.41** on **Hormuz** reopening **hope**, while analysts flagged a **fragile** truce.
- **Strait still not normalized**: **Reuters** shipping sources said **Wednesday** transit **remained shut**, **Iranian navy** threatened ships without **Tehran**’s permission; **Iranian** official told **Reuters** **limited**/**controlled** opening possible **Thursday**/**Friday** ahead of **Pakistan** talks.
- **Lebanon catastrophe**: **Israel** conducted the **heaviest** **Lebanon** strikes of the war **Wednesday**; **Reuters** cited **civil defence**: **254** killed, **>1,100** wounded; **health ministry** **182** dead (**not final**). **AP** led with **182** (**Wednesday**) per **health ministry** after a **correction**. **UN** High Commissioner **Volker Türk** condemned the **scale** of killing.
- **Lebanon not in U.S.–Iran deal (U.S./Israel)**: **Trump** told **PBS** **Lebanon** is **not** part of the ceasefire **“because of Hezbollah”**; **Karoline Leavitt** and **JD Vance** aligned (**Reuters**). **Vance** suggested an **Iranian** **“misunderstanding.”** **Pakistan**’s **Sharif** had publicly linked **Lebanon** to the truce—**contradiction persists**.
- **Iranian** pushback: **Parliament Speaker Mohammad Baqer Qalibaf** posted on **X** that **three** of **ten** conditions were already **breached**—**Lebanon** violence, alleged **post-ceasefire** **drone** over **Iran**, and **U.S.** refusal of **enrichment**—making talks **“unreasonable”** (**Reuters**, **AP**).
- **Hezbollah**: Sources told **Reuters** the group **paused** fire **early Wednesday** then **resumed rockets** **early Thursday** citing **Israeli** violations. **IRGC** warned of **“regret-inducing”** response if **Lebanon** attacks continue (**Reuters**).
- **Regional energy**: **Reuters** industry source: **Iran** hit **Saudi** **East-West Pipeline** hours post-deal; **Gulf** states issued **alerts** (**AP**/**Reuters** threads). **AP**: **Lavan** refinery attack claimed; **UAE** **AD** engaged **incoming** fire.
