# Daily War News Brief - 2026-03-29

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-03-29
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; event times listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments
- Note: 2026-03-29 fell on a **Sunday**; U.S. crude futures did not settle that calendar day. Oil benchmarks below cite the most recent reported weekly settlement (week ending 2026-03-27, Friday) and same-day news context on oil.

## Executive Summary
- **Oil and Hormuz:** Reuters reported **global oil prices surged** on March 29 amid continued fighting. Pakistan-hosted diplomacy centered on **reopening the Strait of Hormuz** (fee structures, possible regional consortium). Pakistan’s foreign minister said **Iran agreed to allow 20 more Pakistani-flagged ships** through Hormuz; AP also reported **Trump said Iran would allow 20 oil-carrying ships through starting Monday** “out of a sign of respect”—treat these as related but not identical formulations until fully reconciled.
- **Diplomacy:** Pakistan announced it would **host U.S.–Iran talks** in the coming days after Saudi, Turkish, and Egyptian foreign ministers met in Islamabad; Egypt’s foreign minister framed the goal as **direct dialogue**. Washington and Tehran did not immediately confirm identical terms in all outlets.
- **Iranian messaging:** Parliament Speaker **Mohammad Bagher Ghalibaf** framed U.S. diplomacy as cover for a **ground invasion**, warned Iranian forces would **“rain fire”** on U.S. troops if they entered, and tied escalation to **Kharg Island**, **Hormuz**, and **nuclear-site** scenarios (CNN analysis quoting his statement).
- **Israel–Lebanon:** Prime Minister **Netanyahu** ordered a **further expansion** of the southern Lebanon “security zone,” citing continued Hezbollah rocket fire (Reuters).
- **Military:** AP reported Israeli jets dropped **more than 120 munitions in Tehran** over 24 hours targeting weapons R&D; parts of **Tehran lost power** after strikes, with state TV later citing **restoration** in affected areas. Israel reported **interceptions** of drones from Yemen early Monday (local time crossing from Sunday reporting).
