# Daily War News Brief - 2026-04-05

## Scope

- Date covered: 2026-04-05
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; **AP** describes the day as **Sunday** (**Easter** in much of the West). Some wire items labeled **“early Monday”** in Iran/Israel local time appear in the same **AP** live file—**calendar boundary** noise noted below.
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary

- **U.S. completed the F-15E crew recovery**: **Trump** and **AP** described a **high-risk CSAR** for the second **Strike Eagle** aviator—**wounded**, hidden in **mountains**—after a **CIA deception** campaign and **dozens** of armed aircraft; **Iran** claimed it **downed** **transports** and **helos** during the mission, while a **regional intelligence** source told **AP** the U.S. **destroyed two transport planes** after **technical failure** (denial/cover **disputed**).
- **Trump** issued **profanity-laden** ultimatums tying **Tuesday** to **Hormuz** reopening, vowing **power plants** and **bridges** and saying Iran would be **“living in Hell”** if the strait stayed closed; he closed one post with **“Praise be to Allah”** (**AP**). **Iran’s U.N. mission** called the rhetoric **evidence of intent to commit war crimes**; **Iran’s joint command** threatened **escalated** strikes on **regional oil and civilian infrastructure** if the **U.S./Israel** hit such targets.
- **Oil repriced higher on the threat headline**: **AP** markets copy said **Sunday early trading** lifted **Brent >2%** to **$111.25** and **U.S. crude nearly 3%** to **$114.54**—first **>$115** neighborhood for **WTI** since **2022** per that item’s framing (**session type**: international / electronic **not** necessarily full U.S. holiday schedule).
- **OPEC+ “paper” hike**: **CNBC** (summarizing **Reuters** sources) said the group might approve a **May** quota increase that would be **mostly theoretical** while **Hormuz** remains shut and **Gulf** members face **war damage**; prior **April +206,000 b/d** noted; **JPMorgan** cited for **>$150** risk if **Hormuz** stays blocked into **mid-May**; **Energy Aspects** called extra barrels **“academic.”**
- **Diplomatic churn**: **Oman** hosted **Iran–Oman** deputy-level talks on **“smooth transit”**; **Egypt**’s FM spoke with **Witkoff**, **Araghchi**, and **Turkey/Pakistan**; **Russia** reported **Lavrov–Araghchi** call; **Bahrain** pushed **U.N.** action on its **Hormuz** draft. **Iran** floated **transit revenue** for **war damages** (**Tabatabaei**) and **Ali Akbar Velayati** warned **Bab el-Mandeb** could be disrupted.
- **Gulf and Israel damage continued**: **Kuwait** reported **power**, **petrochemical**, and **desalination** hits; **UAE** **Khor Fakkan** and **Ruwais** **petchem** fires from **intercept debris**; **Bahrain** **NOC** storage / **petchem** fires; **Haifa** residential strikes and **search** for **survivors** (**AP**). **Lebanon**: **Israeli** strike killed **four** in **Beirut** per health ministry; **LF** official **Pierre Mouawad** killed east of Beirut per **NNA** (**AP** live).
