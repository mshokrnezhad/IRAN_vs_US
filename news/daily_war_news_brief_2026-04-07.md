# Daily War News Brief - 2026-04-07

## Scope

- Date covered: 2026-04-07
- Timezone standard used: **U.S. Tuesday** / **Tehran Wednesday morning** boundary dominates wires: the **two-week ceasefire** was announced **late Tuesday U.S. time** (**<2 hours** before **Trump**’s **8 p.m. EDT** deadline per **Reuters**/**AP**); **AP** also describes **missile alerts** and fighting **“early Wednesday”** in the region—**calendar noise** called out in **Information Gaps**.
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary

- **Trump** agreed to a **two-week ceasefire** with **Iran** **late Tuesday (U.S.)**, **less than two hours** before his **Strait of Hormuz**/**infrastructure** deadline, dropping same-day rhetoric that **“a whole civilization will die tonight”** without a deal (**Reuters**, **AP**).
- **Pakistan** **PM Shehbaz Sharif** said he invited **U.S.** and **Iranian** delegations to **Islamabad** **Friday**; **Trump** cited conversations with **Sharif** and **Gen. Asim Munir**; **Iran**’s **Supreme National Security Council** accepted the truce and said talks would occur in **Pakistan** (**AP**, **Reuters**).
- **Competing narratives**: **Iran**’s **SNSC** framed the outcome as **victory** accepting **Iran**’s conditions; **White House**’s **Karoline Leavitt** called it a **U.S.** victory enabled by military success (**Reuters**). **Netanyahu** said **Israel** backed the **U.S.** pause but the deal **does not cover** **Hezbollah** in **Lebanon**—while **Sharif** was cited (**AP**) for a wider read including **Lebanon**—a **core ambiguity**.
- **Strait / economics**: **Araghchi** said passage would be under **Iranian military management**; **AP** described **Iran**/**Oman** **fee** collection for **reconstruction** per a regional official. **Oil** sold off sharply (**Reuters** linked futures move). **EIA** warned fuel prices could **keep rising for months** even after reopening (**Reuters**).
- **Violence continued around the announcement**: **U.S.** hit **Kharg Island** and **bridges**/**rail**/**petchem** Tuesday; **Gulf** states and **Israel** reported **incoming** fire after **Trump**’s post; **U.S.** official said **offensive** ops **halted** but **defensive** actions continued (**AP**). **Hard-line** protests in **Tehran** condemned compromise (**AP**).
- **Reuters** quoted a **senior Iranian official**: **preconditions** for lasting peace include **halt to strikes**, **non-repetition guarantees**, **compensation**, **rejection of merely temporary** ceasefire, and **Hormuz** **fee** authority varying by ship/cargo.
