# Daily War News Brief - 2026-03-12

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-03-12
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; specific event times are listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary
- Mojtaba Khamenei issued his first public wartime statement, vowing to keep fighting, preserve Hormuz leverage, and possibly open other fronts.
- AP and Reuters reported a dual Iranian message: Mojtaba’s rhetoric favored sustained coercion, while Iran’s U.N. envoy publicly said Tehran was not going to close Hormuz outright.
- Trump issued a fresh threat online, and Netanyahu used his first press conference of the war to argue Israeli attacks were creating conditions for regime collapse in Iran.
- Energy disruption remained central, with oil back near $100, sanctions relief on some Russian oil to ease supply pressure, and continuing attacks on shipping, Gulf infrastructure, and Lebanon.
- AP reported American forces had struck more than 6,000 targets since the war began and that conflict spillover was now hitting French, British, and Iraqi theaters more directly.
- The day marked the clearest articulation yet of Mojtaba Khamenei’s strategy: continued resistance, continued revenge, and continued pressure on U.S. bases and regional energy lifelines.
