# Daily War News Brief - 2026-04-02

## Scope

- Date covered: 2026-04-02
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; **AP** regional copy references **Thursday** (diplomatic meetings, bridge strike framing) and **Friday** dawn in the Gulf—calendar lines blur across zones
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary

- **Oil futures surged**: **May WTI** and front **Brent** jumped roughly **11%** and **~8%** respectively (**CNBC** / **AP**), reversing the prior session’s dip, as markets repriced **prolonged fighting** after Trump’s **Wednesday-night** vow to hit Iran **“extremely hard”** for **two to three weeks** with **no U.S. Hormuz plan** in the same speech.
- **Structure screamed tight near-term supply**: **Reuters** reported **record WTI backwardation**—**May** traded up to **$16.70/barrel** above **June** intraday, with May touching **$113.97** before a settle **~$111.4–111.5** range across wires.
- **Diplomatic Hormuz track split**: the **U.K.** hosted **41 countries** (per **AP**)—**not** including the **U.S.**—to discuss **reopening Hormuz** diplomatically and postwar **mine-clearing / shipping reassurance**; **Macron** called **forced** opening **“unrealistic”** and insisted **coordination with Iran** after a ceasefire. Separately, **IRNA** (via **CNBC**) said **Iran and Oman** were drafting a **transit-monitoring protocol**, briefly **easing** oil and **boosting U.S. equities** off morning lows.
- **Kinetic war stayed hot**: **AP** described **Israel** under **incoming Iranian fire**, **Kuwait** and **Bahrain** under attack, a **U.S. strike** on the **B1 bridge** (Tehran–Karaj) killing **8** and wounding **95** during **Nowruz/Nature Day** gatherings, and **Lebanon** reporting **27 dead** in **24 hours** from Israeli strikes.
- **Pentagon leadership shakeup**: **Hegseth** removed **Army Chief of Staff Gen. Randy George** and **two other** Army generals **without stated cause**; **Gen. Christopher LaNeve** named **acting** chief—**AP** tied the timing to **82nd Airborne** deployments and **no clear** White House **war end date**.
