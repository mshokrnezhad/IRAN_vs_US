# Daily War News Brief - 2026-04-03

## Scope

- Date covered: 2026-04-03
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; **AP** and **Reuters** datelines variously describe the same calendar day as **Friday** (including **Good Friday** in Western markets)
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary

- **First confirmed U.S. manned combat aircraft losses** in the war: **Iran** shot down a U.S. **F-15E** over **Iran**; one **crew member** was **rescued** and a **second remained missing** with **CSAR** under way; **Iranian** TV and officials **urged civilians** to find the aircrew and promised **commendations** for capture or killing. **U.S. officials** also described an **A-10** hit and **crash over Kuwait** with **pilot ejecting**, and **two Black Hawk** helicopters **struck** during rescue but **exiting** Iranian airspace.
- **Energy futures did not trade in the main U.S. session**: **AP** reported **energy markets closed** for **Good Friday** after **Thursday’s** sharp rally (**WTI +11.4%** to **$111.54**, **Brent +7.8%** to **$109.03**); **spot Brent** was described around **$109** in **AP**’s war roundup—still **>50%** above **Feb. 28** levels—so April 3 was a **narrative and physical-risk** day more than a **new futures-print** day.
- **Infrastructure war widened**: **Iran** hit **Kuwait’s Mina al-Ahmadi refinery** and **damaged a desalination plant**; **Kuwait Petroleum Corp.** reported **fires** at the refinery. **Reuters** added **Kuwait** power/water plant reporting and **UAE** **gas-field** shutdown after **debris** from an **intercept**; **missile debris** near **Haifa** refinery area also noted in **Reuters**.
- **Diplomacy stalled**: **Fars** (semi-official), cited by **Reuters**, said **Tehran rejected** a **U.S. 48-hour ceasefire** idea delivered **Wednesday** via an **unnamed third country**; **Wall Street Journal** reporting (via **Reuters**) described **Pakistan-led** ceasefire efforts as at a **dead end** and said **Iran** would **not** meet **U.S.** officials in **Islamabad** imminently.
- **Trump** posted **Hormuz** could be opened to **“TAKE THE OIL, & MAKE A FORTUNE”** with more time, and threatened **bridges** then **power plants**; **Iran’s** **Qalibaf** mocked the war as **downgraded** from **regime change** to **pilot recovery**. **U.N. Security Council** was expected to take up **Hormuz** **Saturday** per **AP**.
