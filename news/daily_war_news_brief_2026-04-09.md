## Executive Summary

- **Netanyahu** said **Israel** would seek **direct talks** with **Beirut** “as soon as possible,” focused on **disarming Hezbollah** and **peaceful relations**—after **Reuters** described **Wednesday**’s **Lebanon** strikes as killing **more than 300** people and placing the **U.S.–Iran** ceasefire **in jeopardy**. **Hezbollah** lawmaker **Ali Fayyad** rejected **direct** talks with **Israel** (**Reuters**).
- **Hormuz** remained a **bottleneck**: **first 24 hours** of ceasefire saw only **one** oil-products tanker and **five** dry-bulk carriers transit vs **~140**/day pre-war (**Reuters**). **Trump** posted that **oil** would flow again and that **Iran** must **not** charge **strait** fees.
- **Iran**’s **Supreme Leader** **Mojtaba Khamenei**—still **not** seen in public—issued a **defiant** **TV-read** statement vowing **punishment** of aggressors and **compensation** for **damage**, and said **Hormuz** management would enter a **“new phase”** (**Reuters**).
- **Israel**/**U.S.** “**forever war**” **doctrine** reporting: **Reuters** described **buffer zones** in **Lebanon**, **Gaza**, and **Syria** as a **semi-permanent** strategy; **Katz**/**Netanyahu** **Mar 31** quotes on **razing** southern **Lebanon** villages **modeled on Gaza** appeared in the **04-09** analysis piece.
- **Pakistan** prepared to host **U.S.–Iran** talks; **Islamabad** security **lockdown** imagery and **Faisal Mosque** dateline **04-09** (**Reuters**). **Pakistani** source said work on **Lebanon** and **Yemen** ceasefires continued (**Reuters**).
- **Lebanon** declared **mourning**; **Health Ministry** toll since **Mar 2** **1,888** dead and **>6,000** wounded (**Reuters**). **Hezbollah** claimed **≥20** military operations **Thursday** (**Reuters**).
