# Daily War News Brief - 2026-03-05

## Scope
- Date covered: 2026-03-05
- Timezone standard used: UTC-based publication timing from reviewed sources; specific event times are listed as reported or `Unknown`
- Focus: Iran / U.S. / Israel conflict and directly connected regional developments

## Executive Summary
- The war widened again on its sixth day, with U.S. and Israeli strikes continuing across Iran, Hezbollah-related escalation deepening in Lebanon, and new spillover involving Gulf states and Azerbaijan.
- Trump said he wanted to be involved in choosing Iran's next leader, openly extending the U.S. role from military destruction into postwar political shaping.
- Hegseth warned that U.S. firepower over Tehran was about to surge dramatically, while AP and Reuters reported major gains claimed by U.S. and Israeli militaries against Iranian air defenses, missile launchers, and ships.
- Iran publicly denied any request for talks with Washington and said the U.S. would bitterly regret sinking the Iranian warship near Sri Lanka.
- Cumulative casualty figures rose sharply again, with AP citing at least 1,230 deaths in Iran, more than 120 in Lebanon, around a dozen in Israel, and six U.S. troops dead.
- Economic and regional disruption intensified further with attacks near U.S. bases, oil infrastructure, shipping lanes, and civilian sites in multiple countries.
